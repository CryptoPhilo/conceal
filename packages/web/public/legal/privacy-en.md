# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-18

## 1. Introduction

Conceal ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Conceal service.

## 2. Information We Collect

**Account information:** Email address and name provided during registration.

**Email metadata:** Sender addresses, subject lines, and timestamps used for filtering and categorization. We request read-only access only.

**Usage data:** Interaction data such as features used, pages visited, and settings configured to improve the Service.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the Service
- Apply your email filter rules and generate daily digests
- Send service notifications (account security, feature updates)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve the Service

## 4. Data Retention

Email metadata used for filtering is processed in real-time and not permanently stored. Account information is retained until you delete your account. Usage data is retained for up to 12 months.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- Service providers who assist in operating the Service (e.g., cloud hosting)
- Legal authorities when required by applicable law

## 6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@conceal.app.

You may revoke email account access at any time by disconnecting your account from the Service.

## 8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences (e.g., language preference stored in NEXT_LOCALE cookie). We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice in the Service.

## 10. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at privacy@conceal.app.
